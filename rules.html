rules

don't hack/cheat

Hacking is defined on xinabox.us to be any client-side modification that causes an unfair advantage towards player not using that modification. This can range from x-ray to aimbot. If you are caught using any one of these hacks by one of our staff members, you will be permenantly banned.

don't spam

Spam is considered the fast repition of the same message three times in a row. It is very annoying to all other players and will result in mutes of different amounts of time. If you want to get a point across, just simply say what you want once without repeating it multiple times.

don't advertise

Advertising is defined on xinabox as putting server ips or names in the chat. If you do so, you will be permanently muted. If you want a friend to join a certain server, simply use /msg and then your friend's name.

no ddos threats

ANY DDoS threat on ANY server, regardless of context, will result in a PERMANENT BAN from all servers. Never threaten to DDoS anywhere, not just because you will be banned permanently, but because it is also illegal. If you are mad at someone, try to solve the problem with words or, if it is serious, request one of our staff members for help.
